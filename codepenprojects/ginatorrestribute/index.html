Gina Torres

Sci-Fi Queen

Gina Torres in Suits

Highlights of Her Career:


  • 1995: Her breakout roles as Magdelena and Nell on One Life to Live
  • 1997: Her appearance as Cleopatra in Xena: Warrior Princess
  • 1997-1999: Recurring role of Nebula in Hercules: The Legendary Journeys
  • 2000-2001: She won an Alma Award for her portrayal of Helen "Hel" Carter in Cleopatra 2525
  • 2002-2003,2005: As one of the leads as Zoe Washburne in Firefly/Serenity
  • 2003: She was nominated for a Satellite Award for Best Supporting Actress for her portrayal of Jasmine in Angel
  • 2011: Her recurring role of Julia Milliken in 24
  • 2011-2018: She was nominated for an ALMA Award for Favorite Supporting Actress on Television for her role of Jessica Pearson in Suits
  • 2013-2015: She was nominated for a Saturn Award for Best Guest Starring Role on Television for her role of Phyllis "Bella" Crawford in Hannibal
